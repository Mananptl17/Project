About Us

COMPANY PROFILE

Automobili Lamborghini S.p.A., founded in 1963 and headquartered in Sant'gata Bolognese, Northern Italy, is renowned for producing iconic super sports cars. The current lineup includes the V12-powered Aventador (launched in 2011), the V10-powered Huracán (launched in 2014), and the Urus Super SUV, powered by a twin-turbo V8 engine (launched in 2017).

Over its history of more than half a century, Lamborghini has crafted a series of dream cars, such as the 350 GT, Miura, Espada, Countach, Diablo, and Murciélago, as well as exclusive limited editions like the Reventón, Sesto Elemento, Veneno, and Centenario.

In 2019, Lamborghini unveiled the Sián FKP 37, a hybrid innovation with a limited production of only 63 units, showcasing groundbreaking hybrid technologies, including the world's first application of a supercapacitor, advanced materials, and unmatched performance that epitomize Lamborghini's legacy.
